My way to ServiceNow...

I am currently working at GuideVision Germany, a Premier ServiceNow Partner, in Munich as a Principal ServiceNow Consultant and Architect.

Born in 1975, I started my career at debis Systemhaus (bought by T-Systems in 2001) in 2000 as a Fachinformatiker for system integration.

In 2002 I moved on to be a software developer at AME-Systemberatung for 3 years, until I decided to start my own little company n.o.standard (2005-2007) and worked as a independant software developer.

In late 2007 Serena Software became my employer for 5 years where I became an ITSM and ALM consultant.

In 2013 ServiceNow became the focus of my working life: I started at ServiceNow as a Senior ITSM Technical Consultant and team lead for a little more than 4 years, taking over technical lead role in several ITSM and ITOM projects.

From August 2017 to November 2018 I worked as ServiceNow Platform Architect in the digital ressort for the Hoffmann-Group in Munich, enjoying the ServiceNow customer side.

In December 2018 I returned to ServiceNow consulting, joining Media Solutions as a Senior ServiceNow consultant extending my knowledge in Service Portals, Virtual Agent, Predictive Intelligence and more.

Since November 2019 I am Principal ServiceNow Consultant and Architect at GuideVision Germany.

I love sports, riding my e-scooter as well as my electric SmartEQ, listening to podcasts, read a good (scifi) book and vegan cooking.
